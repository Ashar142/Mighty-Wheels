Our Models

SPORT BIKE

Sport bikes are high-performance motorcycles designed for agility and speed, featuring a forward-leaning riding position and aerodynamic styling. They excel on twisting roads and racetracks, delivering sharp handling and powerful acceleration.

DIRT BIKE

Dirt bikes are lightweight motorcycles designed for off-road riding, featuring rugged tires, high suspension travel, and a compact frame. They excel on rough terrain, including trails, dirt tracks, and obstacles, providing agility and control in challenging conditions.

CRUISER BIKE

Cruisers have a low, often wide seat, allowing for a comfortable riding position and easy foot placement.Handlebars are positioned higher and further back, with foot pegs placed forward, creating an upright or slightly leaned-back posture.
